About Ekhata

Ekhata is an accounting application developed by BBIS Studnet for thier 2nd year project . Designed primarily for small and medium-sized businesses, it provides a range of financial and business management tools such as:

  • Bookkeeping
  • Accounting
  • Expense Tracking
  • Financial Reporting
  • Tax Preparation
